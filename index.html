At The Cinema

(Quick Reviews)

When I saw the trailer for The Foreigner I thought it was going to be 2 hours of Jackie Chan beating the crap out of people. In reality the film isn't really about Jackie going on a rampage while looking fo answers. Its really about Pierce Brosnan a 'former' IRA member turned politican trying to make political moves while this rightfully so, madman Jackie looms over him. The plot wasn't too convulted like political dramas tend to be. It was pretty cool to see Jackie fighting a bit dirty, smashing people through tables and cracking bottles over heads. The film was very chatty though and after the conflict was resolved it dragged for a bit. I'd suggest catching this one on tv if you like. But if you were looking for pure action you're better of watching a montage of the fights on youtube.
